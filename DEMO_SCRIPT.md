# GovCert HeyGen Demo Script

## Production Details
- **Duration**: ~4-5 minutes
- **Avatar**: Professional male or female presenter (business casual)
- **Tone**: Confident, empathetic, authoritative — founder speaking to fellow business owners
- **Background**: Clean office or transparent overlay on product screenshots
- **Music**: Subtle, modern, corporate underscore (low volume)

---

## SCENE 1: THE PAIN (0:00 - 0:45)
**Visual**: Dark background with scattered documents — tax returns, capability statements, org charts, emails, spreadsheets flying across screen. Text overlays appear as the narrator speaks.

**NARRATOR**:

"If you've ever applied for a government certification — 8(a), GSA Schedule, WOSB, HUBZone — you know the pain.

The information you need? It exists. It's on your desk. It's in your inbox. It's buried in your Google Drive. Your tax returns are with your accountant. Your org chart is in a PowerPoint from last year. Your past performance references are scattered across three email threads.

And once you finally find everything? You have to read it, analyze it, reorganize it, and rewrite it into the specific language that each application demands."

**Visual**: Clock animation showing weeks turning into months. Calendar pages flipping.

"At House Strategies Group, our government contracting firm, we spent weeks — sometimes months — piecing together a single certification application. We did this for ourselves. We did it for our clients. Every time, we asked the same question:"

**Visual**: Text appears in gold on navy background: *"There has to be an easier way."*

## SCENE 2: THE ANSWER (0:45 - 1:15)
**Visual**: Transition to the GovCert landing page — the hero image with the Capitol Building, "Certifications that open doors" headline, $700B+ / 170K+ / 80% stats bar.

**NARRATOR**:

"GovCert is the answer.

We built GovCert because AI has finally made it possible to do what we always wished we could — take your raw documentation and transform it into review-ready certification applications. Quickly. Accurately. At a quality level that used to take a team of consultants weeks to produce."

**Visual**: Zoom into "Start Free Trial" button.

"Let me show you exactly how it works. This is a real walkthrough — everything you're about to see is live."

---

## SCENE 3: SIGN UP & ELIGIBILITY (1:15 - 1:50)
**Visual**: Show the Registration page — 3-step flow: Choose Plan, Create Account, Verify Email.

**NARRATOR**:

"First, you create your account. Takes about 30 seconds."

**Visual**: Cut to the Eligibility Wizard — Step 1: Business Basics. Show the fields being filled in: Business Name, EIN, Entity Type, Year Established, NAICS codes.

"Once you're in, GovCert launches the Eligibility Wizard. You answer a few questions about your business — the basics: your entity type, when you were established, your NAICS codes."

**Visual**: Quick cuts through the wizard steps — Ownership & Demographics (owners with percentages), Financials (revenue fields + QuickBooks connect button), Location (HUBZone ZIP check).

"Ownership demographics, financials — and if you use QuickBooks, you can connect it right here and we'll pull your revenue data automatically. No manual entry."

**Visual**: Step 5 — Past Performance (SAM.gov, contracts), Step 6 — Document Upload showing the drag-and-drop area.

"You tell us about your federal registrations and past contracts. Then upload your key documents — financial statements, tax returns, capability statement."

**Visual**: Show a document being uploaded, then the AI analysis appearing: category badge, year tag, usefulness score, key data points.

"Watch this — the moment you upload a document, GovCert's AI reads it, categorizes it, extracts the year, and identifies the key data points. That tax return? We know it's for fiscal year 2024. That financial statement? We've already pulled your revenue and net income."

## SCENE 4: ELIGIBILITY RESULTS (1:50 - 2:15)
**Visual**: Show the "Run Eligibility Assessment" button being clicked, then the loading spinner, then the Results page with certification cards showing ELIGIBLE / LIKELY ELIGIBLE / NEEDS REVIEW badges.

**NARRATOR**:

"Now here's where it gets exciting. Click 'Run Eligibility Assessment' and in seconds, GovCert analyzes everything you've provided against the actual requirements for every major certification."

**Visual**: Pan across the results — 8(a), GSA MAS, WOSB, EDWOSB, HUBZone, SDVOSB, VOSB, plus state certifications. Each shows a status badge and a plain-English AI summary.

"You get a clear, instant answer: which certifications you qualify for, which ones you're close on, and exactly what's missing. No guesswork. No $5,000 consultant just to tell you where you stand."

**Visual**: Zoom into one of the AI summaries showing the specific reasoning.

"Each assessment comes with a plain-English explanation — not legal jargon. You'll know exactly why you qualify, or what you need to fix."

---

## SCENE 5: APPLICATION DASHBOARD (2:15 - 2:45)
**Visual**: Show the GSA MAS Certification Dashboard — the SIN selection wizard with checkboxes, then the Application Sections list (Corporate Experience, Quality Control Plan, Project Experience per SIN, Past Performance, Financials, Pricing, Submit).

**NARRATOR**:

"Ready to apply? GovCert builds your entire application dashboard. For a GSA Schedule, you start by selecting your SIN codes — the service categories you want to offer."

**Visual**: Show SINs being selected, then the dashboard populating with sections.

"Instantly, your application breaks down into every section you need to complete. Corporate Experience. Quality Control Plan. Project Experience for each SIN. Past Performance references. Financials. Pricing. And your final submission package."

**Visual**: Quick highlight of the progress percentage and completion badges.

"You can see your progress at a glance — what's done, what's pending, what needs attention."

## SCENE 6: AI-POWERED NARRATIVES (2:45 - 3:20)
**Visual**: Show the Corporate Experience page — the "Generate All Sections" button. Show the AI generating all 8 narrative sections simultaneously with a loading animation.

**NARRATOR**:

"Here's where GovCert saves you weeks of work. Click one button, and our AI drafts your entire Corporate Experience narrative — company overview, capabilities, employee qualifications, organizational controls, resources, past projects, marketing plan, and subcontractor management."

**Visual**: Show the generated text appearing in the editor — professional, specific narrative text. Show the character counter tracking against GSA's limit.

"This isn't generic filler. GovCert uses everything it knows about your business — your eligibility data, your uploaded documents, your financial records — to write specific, professional narratives tailored to your company."

**Visual**: Show the Quality Control Plan page with similar AI generation, then a Project Experience page with the document upload + voice input options.

"Same thing for your Quality Control Plan. Same thing for each Project Experience narrative. You can upload a past proposal, speak into your microphone, or just describe your work in a few sentences — GovCert shapes it into GSA-compliant language."

**Visual**: Show the character counter at 8,400/10,000 — well within GSA's limit.

"And it stays within GSA's 10,000-character limit automatically."

---

## SCENE 7: PAST PERFORMANCE & PPQ (3:20 - 3:50)
**Visual**: Show the Past Performance page with contract cards. Show clicking "Send PPQ Request" and the 3-step modal appearing.

**NARRATOR**:

"Past performance references are usually the most painful part of any certification. You need at least three, and you need them to fill out formal questionnaires."

**Visual**: Show Step 1 of the PPQ modal — filling in reference name and email. Show the AI drafting a personalized email.

"GovCert makes it painless. Enter your reference's name and email, tell us how you worked together, and our AI drafts a personalized email requesting the questionnaire. You review it, edit if you want, then send — all from inside the platform."

**Visual**: Show the PPQ responder page — the clean form with ratings, narratives, and the canvas signature pad with a hand-drawn signature.

"Your reference gets a polished, easy-to-complete form. Ratings, narrative questions, and an electronic signature pad. When they submit, GovCert automatically generates a signed PDF, stores it in your documents, and emails you to let you know it's done."

**Visual**: Show the PPQ status badges — SENT, OPENED, COMPLETED in green.

"You can track every reference in real time. Sent. Opened. Completed."

---

## SCENE 8: PRICING & CSP-1 (3:50 - 4:10)
**Visual**: Show the Pricing/CSP-1 page — labor categories being added, the AI library search, rate benchmarking.

**NARRATOR**:

"For GSA Schedule, you also need a Commercial Supplier Pricelist — your CSP-1. GovCert's pricing builder lets you add labor categories, benchmark your rates against market data, and run a gap analysis against your SINs to make sure you're not missing anything."

**Visual**: Show the "Export CSP-1" button and CSV downloading.

"When you're done, export it as a formatted CSV — ready to upload directly into GSA eOffer."

---

## SCENE 9: EOFFER SUBMISSION (4:10 - 4:35)
**Visual**: Show the eOffer Submission Package page — the paywall unlocking (Free during beta), then the full submission guide with tabs, copy buttons, and the completeness score.

**NARRATOR**:

"Finally — submission day. GovCert maps every piece of your application to the exact field in GSA's eOffer system. Tab by tab, field by field."

**Visual**: Show clicking "Copy" next to a narrative field, green checkmark appearing. Show the CSP-1 download section with the LCAT table. Show the editable company info section.

"Copy each field and paste it directly into eOffer. Download your CSP-1. Upload your PPQ documents. Your company information is editable right here if anything needs a last-minute update."

**Visual**: Show the "Open GSA eOffer" button and the 100% completeness score.

"When every field shows green, you're done. Open eOffer, paste, submit. What used to take months now takes days."

**Visual**: Return to the landing page hero. Stats bar: $700B+ Federal Contracts / 170K+ Certified Businesses / 80% Faster with GovCert.

"GovCert is that way. AI-powered. Built by government contractors, for government contractors."
